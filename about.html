Who is Meechie?

My name is Christian “SWOLE” Ricketts, a fitness trainer, content creator, and gymrat from Kansas City, MO with an ambition to inspire, motivate, and supplement YOU with your fitness goals.
My journey began as a high school athlete aspiring to play at the next level. When things went left, I got right, and allowed fitness to change my life for the better.
I strive to help my clients better themselves and begin the journey towards their dream bodies, while making fitness Enjoyable and manageable.
